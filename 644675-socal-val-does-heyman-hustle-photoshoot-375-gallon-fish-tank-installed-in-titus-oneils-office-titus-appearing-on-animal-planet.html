375 Gallon Fish Tank Installed in Titus O’Neil’s Office

As noted, WWE star Titus O’Neil was recently filming at the WWE Performance Center for an upcoming episode of Animal Planet’s show “Tanked.”

The crew finished shooting the episode on Friday, and they installed a 4,000 pound, 375-gallon saltwater fish tank in Titus’ office. The episode will air in April 2016.

According to TBO.com, O’Neil owns a sports and entertainment management company in Lutz, Florida called Two Toros, and this is where the tank was installed.

O’Neil had the following to say about having the Florida Gators themed tank installed in his office:

“I don’t let a lot of people into my home. I want people to experience it and my office is where I conduct much of my business.”
