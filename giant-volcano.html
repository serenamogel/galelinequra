Giant Volcano (Japanese: きょだいかざん Giant Volcano) is a dungeon in Pokémon Mystery Dungeon: Explorers of Time and Explorers of Darkness and Pokémon Mystery Dungeon: Explorers of Sky in the Volcanic Area. Heatran guards the Fiery Drum at Giant Volcano Peak (Japanese: きょだいかざん ちょうじょう). The Pokémon found here are very similar to those found in the Dark Crater.

Password

In Explorers of Time and Darkness, Wonder Mail password is the only way to unlock this dungeon; in Explorers of Sky it can be unlocked via Spinda's Café. Once the player has cleared Crevice Cave and obtained Secret Rank, the following codes can unlock this dungeon.

Gameplay

The jobs are ranked E or ★2 through ★6 by default, depending on the floor.

The dungeon uses following layouts:

  • Medium-sized floors with 3-8 rooms on floors 1, 2 and 3
  • Large-sized floors with 3-8 rooms on other floors
  • Large-sized floors with 3-12 rooms on floors 16, 17 and 19
  • Floors 6, 11 and 18 have a giant room in middle with smaller rooms on left and right.

There's 3-6 items on the ground and 6-9 in the walls. There's 2-3 starting enemies on floors 1 through 4, 2-4 on floors 5 through 11, and 3-5 on floors 12 through 19. There is a 10% chance each for a room on every fourth floor, starting from 1, to be filled with lava.

The IQ Booster activates on every floor. Turn limit is 1000. Enemies in the dungeon have 331IQ, including Heatran. There is lava in the dungeon on floors 3, 5, 7, 8, 11, 13, 15, 16 and 19 (on top of the lava that might occasionally spawn in the rooms). The items in this dungeon spawn sticky 5% of the time.

Weather on floors 3 and 5 is sunny, while on floors 6, 8 and 10 is cloudy.

Like other Seven Treasures dungeons other than Sky Stairway, stat boosting items and Lost Loot is buried in the walls (in contrast to most dungeons which only have Poké).

Pokémon encountered

Each time the player enters floors 1 through 8, only 14 of the species that can be found on a given floor will be chosen. The unpicked Pokémon's spawn rate will be added to whichever picked Pokémon is next in the National Dex order, going back to the beginning if needed.
