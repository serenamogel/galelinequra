Is Claire Holt still married?

 Posted on April 1, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  263 words  |  Larita Shotwell

Claire Rhiannon Holt is an Australian actress. She is known for her roles as Emma Gilbert in H2O: Just Add Water, Samara Cook in Pretty Little Liars, Chastity Meyer in Mean Girls 2, Rebekah Mikaelson in The Vampire Diaries, and its spinoff series The Originals and Legacies and Kate in the 2017 survival horror film, 47 Meters Down.

Holt was born in Brisbane to Geoffrey Holt and Ann. She has two sisters, Rachael (born in 1987) and Madeline (born in 1992), and one brother, David (born in 1989).

Is Claire Holt still married?

Holt initially married her long-term lover, television producer Matt Kaplan, in July 2015. They married in April 2016.

However, on 27 April 2017, Kaplan filed for divorce from Holt, citing “irreconcilable disagreements”. Holt subsequently submitted a response on May 2, 2017, also requesting that her name be changed from Kaplan to Holt.

Holt then announced her engagement to a real estate executive Andrew Joblon on December 3, 2017. On March 5, 2018, Holt announced on Instagram that she had miscarried. Holt and Joblon married on August 18, 2018.

On 11 October 2018, Holt announced on Instagram that she was pregnant, and revealed on 19 November that she was expecting a son. On 28 March 2019, she gave birth to a son. On 3 April 2020, Holt announced on Instagram that she was expecting her second child, and revealed on 16 April 2020 that she was expecting a girl. On 12 September 2020, Holt gave birth to a daughter.

Holt announced that she became an American citizen in November 2019
