Musa Mseleku New Wife: Is He Really Looking for a Fifth Spouse?

 Posted on July 19, 2024  |  4 minutes  |  772 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

Musa Mseleku, a prominent South African entrepreneur and reality TV personality, is renowned for his polygamous lifestyle, having four wives and fathering 10 children.

He is a prominent figure on the popular television show “Uthando Nes’thembu,” which provides insights into his family life.

However, in 2019, he surprised numerous viewers by revealing his intention to find a fifth wife.

He extended an invitation to interested women to apply for a dinner date with him through an event named “In Pursuit of Number.”

The occasion garnered the presence of 20 women, each paying R5,000 to share a meal with the prosperous polygamist.

The question lingers: Did he encounter his fifth wife within this group?

Moreover, how do his existing wives perceive his quest?

This article offers an in-depth look into Musa Mseleku’s pursuit of a new wife.

Who is Faith Duma?

Among the attendees of the dinner date with Musa Mseleku was Faith Duma, a young and exquisite woman hailing from Durban and currently residing in Johannesburg.

She stands as the founder of an organization named Women Empowered by Faith, aiming to instill confidence and courage in women, urging them to live purposefully in alignment with their faith.

Faith Duma is also recognized as a radio personality and a motivational speaker.

Faith Duma triggered speculations about her being Musa Mseleku’s prospective new wife after sharing an Instagram photo alongside him, devoid of any caption.

Numerous fans presumed that she had won his affections, while others theorized her to be a sibling or relative.

Yet, neither Musa nor Faith officially acknowledged or refuted their relationship status, keeping many in suspense.

How do Musa Mseleku’s wives react to his search for a fifth wife?

Musa Mseleku’s wives differ on his pursuit of a fifth spouse.

MaCele opposes it, prioritizing their current family and fearing neglect.

MaYeni shares her opposition, seeing it as unjust and disregarding their culture.

MaKhumalo supports it, empathizing with his desire for more children.

MaNgwabe is open to the idea if the new wife follows their marriage rules.

She hopes the fifth wife brings harmony to the family.

Conclusion

Musa Mseleku’s pursuit of a new wife remains shrouded in mystery for curious fans intrigued by his polygamous lifestyle.

He has alluded to revealing her identity on his reality TV show in the near future.

Whether this role is assumed by Faith Duma or another individual, only time will unveil the manner in which Musa Mseleku’s new wife integrates into his family, and how his existing wives extend their welcome.
