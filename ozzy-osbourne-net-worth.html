Ozzy Osbourne: Career

Ozzy Osbourne’s musical career journey started in 1967.

He joined a Geezer Butler’s band called Rare Breed, which later became the legendary band Black Sabbath.

Then the Butler and Osbourne recruited other members of the band.

After a while, the band released their self-titled debut album on February 13, 1970, which amassed millions of dollars from the album sales.

In addition, Ozzy and the band dropped their second album on September 18, 1970.

This one was entitled Paranoid, and it took this heavy metal band to the pinnacle of success.

As of this writing, Black Sabbath has dropped 19 studio albums in which all the vocals are provided by the British singer Ozzy Osbourne.

Aside from a band career, he has also gained a name as a solo artist since he has released numerous outstanding tracks and albums.

Osbourne has released over twelve solo studio albums, five live albums, nine music videos, and five extended plays (EP) as of October 2021.

In addition, Ozzy Osbourne also appeared in MTV’s reality show called The Osbournes.

To conclude, this show has 4 seasons with 52 episodes as of this writing and preparing to air a new season in early 2022.
