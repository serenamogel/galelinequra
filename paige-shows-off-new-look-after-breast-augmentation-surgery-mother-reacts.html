Paige Shows Off New Look After Breast Augmentation Surgery Mother Reacts

 Posted on August 31, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  225 words  |  Aldo Pusey

Paige is very proud of who she is. There is nothing to hide and she’s an open book. Now she is showing off after a recent breast augmentation surgery.

Her mother Saraya Knight tweeted out how proud she is of her daughter. Paige has been through so much and she is a survivor.

She was 18 years old when she moved to usa for @WWE, she is 27 now, and she has also been to hell and back and HAD to have work done…. my daughter is amazing and I would rather her look this way than seeing her broken. She’s a survivor and she is now happy, She is beautiful

Paige’s recent breast augmentation surgery caused quite a stir. When PW Stream questioned if she had work done some seemed to take offense. It turns out that Paige did have surgery and no, she has absolutely nothing to be ashamed of either.

We’re happy for Paige and the fact that she’s living her best life.
