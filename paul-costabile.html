Paul Costabile is an American TV show host of the Back Pop Up show. Apart from that, he is a multitalented person who is involved in the entertainment industry as a comedian, producer, writer, and actor.

In addition, he is well known among the public as the husband of the American singer and songwriter Christina Perri.

Relationship Status: Who Is Costabile's Wife?

As mentioned above, Paul Costabile is currently in a married relationship with his long-time partner, Christina Perri. His wife Christina is a singer and songwriter well known among the public for his romantic and melody songs.

The romantic couple first met during a mid- interview at iHeartRadio held in 2013. After knowing each other for a while, they began dating the same year.

On June 22, 2017, Paul proposed to Christina for marriage, and obviously, the answer was yes. He right away made a post on his Instagram handle captioning,

I knew I wanted to ask this girl to marry me the day she walked into the green screen for an interview almost four years ago. I finally asked her right here tonight, and she said yes !!!

On December 12, 2017, they tied their knot in a wedding ceremony in New York City.

Currently, the couple is enjoying their married life and are living happily with their daughter in their house in New York City, New York, United States.

Costabile Became A Father In 2018

They welcomed a daughter, and the couple named the cute baby Carmella Stanley Costabile.

Costabile is taking good care of her daughter, Carmella, and incredibly fulfilling his role as a father. He often shares photos of his cute daughter on his Instagram handle.

Costabile's Wife Christina Suffered a Miscarriage

We are shocked and completely heartbroken. We were only one week away from sharing the news, so I feel it’s also important to share this news.

I am so sad but not discouraged. When the time feels right we will try again, but today, we mourn our little life lost.
