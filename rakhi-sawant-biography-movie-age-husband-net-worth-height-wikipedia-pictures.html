Early Life & Education

Rakhi Sawant, born Neeru Bheda, is also known as Fatima Durrani. She was born on November 25, 1976, in Agra, Uttar Pradesh, India, and she is now 47 years old as of 2023.

Her stepfather, Anand Sawant, raised her and later took up her stepfather’s surname.

Her mother, on the other hand, is simply identified as Maya. It is unclear whether Rakhi is their parent’s only child, as she has not revealed any details concerning her siblings.

Similarly, she has not revealed anything concerning her educational qualifications to the general public.

Personal Life

Rakhi Sawant has had a colorful romantic relationship history. She was previously married to Ritesh Singh, but the marriage ended in divorce in 2022.

In May 2022, she tied the knot with her boyfriend of a few months, Adil Khan Durrani. There are no reports of Rakhi having any kids.

However, she revealed that she considers her students as her kids.

Career

Rakhi Sawant’s career began in 1997 when she made her film debut in the movie “Agnichakra” under the name Ruhi Sawant.

She then appeared in several Bollywood films, including “Joru Ka Ghulam” and “Main Hoon Na.” In 2003, she gained recognition for item number “Mohabbat Hai Mirchi” in the film “Chura Liyaa Hai Tumne.”

She has also participated in reality shows like “Bigg Boss” and “Nach Baliye,” where she showcased her dancing skills.

Rakhi Sawant has hosted her talk show called “Rakhi Ka Insaaf” and has appeared in various other television shows.
