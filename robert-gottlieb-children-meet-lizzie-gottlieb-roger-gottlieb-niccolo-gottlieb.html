Robert Gottlieb who was an editor seems to have rubbed off his skills on his children.

He has three kids who are all talented and accomplished in their own right. They are a testament to their father’s love of literature and the arts.

Robert Gottlieb children: Meet Lizzie Gottlieb, Roger Gottlieb, Niccolò Gottlieb

Roger Gottlieb, first child of Robert from his first marriage, is a professor of environmental studies at Occidental College. He is the author of several books, including The New Geopolitics of Climate Change and A Life of Crime: How Politics Became a Business.

Lizzie Gottlieb, Robert’s child from his second marriage, is a documentary filmmaker. Her films have been shown at film festivals around the world. She is the director of the films Today’s Man and Turn Every Page.

Also, Niccolò Gottlieb is a writer and musician. He is the author of the book The Book of Niccolò. He is also a member of the band The Arcs.
