Who is Saucy Santana?

Saucy Santana was born Justin Harris in Tallahassee Florida on October 8, 1993. He was raised in Tallahassee by his mother, Teresa Harris. There is not much detail available about his early life and background.

The rapper’s career is still growing but notwithstanding, his income has increased significantly. All the money he makes is from his music career. His exact net worth is not available at the moment.

Here Are 10 Interesting Facts about Saucy Santana

1. Saucy Santana’s Career

The rapper developed an interest in music when he was a young child. After his first song got out in august 2019, he gained more recognition and fans. So far, he has released two albums Pretty Little Gangsta, and Imam Celebrity, and one EP Dog Walkers. Some of his famous songs include Hood Nigga, Bang, Gold Digger, Get It, Let Him Go, Get That Bag, Try Your Luck, Trap Girl, and a few others. Also, he has worked with record labels like StreamCut, Arena Music Productions, LLC, etc.

2. He has a YouTube channel

Saucy Santana runs a YouTube channel where he uploads several videos related to music and fun. He has about 170k subscribers and has as high as 3.9 million views on a video.

3. Saucy Santana’s Height, Weight, and other body Measurements

Saucy Santana has an average height. He has a full-sized body which he is always ready to flaunt on social media. His height and weight have not been provided but the rapper sure doesn’t look short or slim.

4. Social media presence

6. He was shot in 2019

In 2019, the rapper was shot outside a stripper club near interstate 95 and Miami Gardens drive with some of his friends. Before the shooting Saucy Santana was partying with his friends at the Office Gentlemen’s Club, 250 NE 183rd St., in North Miami Dade.

The rapper suggested that he was shot because of the type of artist he is. He was shot in the arm and luckily for him, the paramedics were there on time to help treat and stabilize his gunshot wound. His mother, Teresa Harris claimed the act was done by those who envied her son’s progress in the entertainment world. The shooting was investigated by the Florida Highway Patrol.

7. Saucy Santana revealed his sexuality when he was 17

Saucy Santana did not reveal that he was gay until he was 17 years old. He said he was very girly even before coming out. He was always wearing girl clothes, purses, and even makeup. He added that he didn’t care what people might think of him.

8. He wants to be an example for aspiring LGBTQ rappers

Saucy Santana revealed in an interview that he intends to lead with a good example for the upcoming LGBTQ rappers. He intends to pave the way for other artists like him. He believes the LGBTQ community will have a major impact on the industry.

9. Saucy Santana is inspired by some famous artists

The rapper revealed that he is inspired by Florida rappers Jackie, Khia, and Trina. He added that his all-time favorite is Gucci Mane. Santana revealed he knows all Gucci Mane songs ever released. Aside from that, he has an addiction to Chanel bags.

10. His stage name

Saucy Santana mentioned that his stage name was given to him by some boys from his hood. He said they were usually referring to him as Santana while his cousin always referred to his looks as saucy so he decided to combine both.
