Sophia Taylor Ali is an American actress. She was born on 7th November 1995 in San Diego, California. She belongs to a Pakistani Muslim family. She is best known for her outstanding acting performance in the MTV romantic comedy series, Faking It, the ABC medical drama series Grey’s Anatomy and the 2018 horror film Truth or Dare.
