Tha Chill is an American rapper and record producer who is a co-founder of the Compton-based West Coast hip hop/gangsta rap group named Compton’s Most Wanted, along with frontman Aaron “MC Eiht” Tyler. Compton’s Most Wanted has released many albums and tracks such as It’s a Compton Thang, Music to Driveby, Gangsta Bizness, Straight Checkn ‘Em, Represent, One Time Gaffled ‘Em Up, Hood Took Me Under, Growin’ Up In the Hood, and I’m Wit Dat. In addition to that, Tha Chill has also worked as a solo artist and has released various albums, extended plays, mixtapes, and songs like Tha Wind Chill Factor, Chillafornia, Chillstrumental, Fohead, 4Wit80, and Chillafornia EP.
